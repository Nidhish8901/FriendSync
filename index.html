How to Get Started

1

Create or Join a Group

Enter a unique name to create a new group, or type in an existing Group ID to join friends who have already set one up.

2

Select Your Group

From your dashboard, simply click on any group from your list. This will take you to that group's shared schedule view.

3

Set Your Schedule

Upload an ICS calendar file for a quick import, or manually click and drag on the timetable to mark your busy hours. Your schedule is saved globally across all your groups!

4

Find the Perfect Time

Use the "Check Availability" tools to instantly see when a specific friend is free, or find out which friends are available at a time that works for you.

About FriendSync

FriendSync is a simple tool designed to make scheduling with friends, family, and teammates a breeze. No more endless back-and-forth messages! Just create a group, set your availability using the timetable or by importing an ICS file, and instantly see when everyone is free.
